# About Me

I am a Ph.D. student (1st year) at the Life Engineering Laboratory, University of Tsukuba.

My research focuses on developing novel haptic and thermal interfaces for immersive virtual reality experiences.

I am interested in understanding human perception mechanisms and applying perceptual phenomena to create new interaction technologies.

# Research Activities

My research explores how human perceptual mechanisms can be utilized to create immersive experiences.

In particular, I investigate:

- Thermal feedback methods for virtual reality
- Warmth motion illusion induced by pulling illusion
- Non-contact thermal presentation
- Vibrotactile-based perceptual modulation
